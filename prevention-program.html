HIV & Viral Hepatitis Prevention Programs

Empowering communities to prevent infections and live healthier lives.

HIV Awareness Campaigns

Raising awareness about HIV prevention, treatment, and stigma reduction through:

  • Community-based workshops
  • Mass media campaigns
  • School-based education

HIV Testing Services

Offering confidential and free HIV testing services to encourage early diagnosis and treatment.

Prevention of Mother-to-Child Transmission (PMTCT)

Providing antiretroviral therapy, counseling, and support for pregnant women living with HIV to prevent transmission to their babies.

Viral Hepatitis Prevention

Reducing the spread of Hepatitis B and C through:

  • Vaccination campaigns
  • Safe blood transfusion practices
  • Harm reduction programs for injecting drug users
