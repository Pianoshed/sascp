Gender and Human Rights for Key Populations and PLHIV in Ondo State

Introduction

In Ondo State, we uphold the rights of key populations and People Living with HIV (PLHIV). Gender equality and human rights are central to our efforts in combating HIV and ensuring that all individuals have access to appropriate care, testing, counseling, and legal protection. Our mission is to provide a safe environment where marginalized groups, including MSM, WSW, AGYW, and others, can seek support and know their rights.

Legal Backings and Platforms Upholding Human Rights

There are various legal frameworks in Nigeria that ensure the protection of human rights, especially in the context of HIV/AIDS. These include:

  • The Nigerian Constitution: Guarantees the fundamental rights of every citizen, including the right to health, dignity, and equality.
  • National HIV/AIDS Policy: This policy focuses on reducing the impact of HIV on society, protecting the rights of PLHIV, and ensuring equitable access to treatment.
  • The African Charter on Human and Peoples' Rights: A regional human rights instrument that Nigeria is a signatory to, ensuring the protection of rights of individuals and groups, including vulnerable populations.
  • The Criminal Code and Penal Code (Nigeria): These codes prohibit discrimination, violence, and harm against individuals based on their sexual orientation or gender identity.

Additionally, there are various international platforms advocating for human rights, including UNAIDS, PEPFAR, and the Global Fund, which work to promote access to HIV prevention, treatment, and care worldwide.

Report Human Rights Violations

If you or someone you know has experienced a violation of rights related to HIV/AIDS or gender-based discrimination, we encourage you to report it. Your voice matters in ensuring that such violations do not go unnoticed. You can report any incidents to the following:

  • Ondo State Human Rights Commission: Call 0800-HRC-OND (0800-472-663)
  • Ondo State AIDS Control Program (OSACP): Email osacp@ondostate.gov.ng
  • National Human Rights Commission: Call 0703-443-8821 or visit the website: www.nhrc.gov.ng

Alternatively, use the Report a violation to reach out to our team to report a violation.
