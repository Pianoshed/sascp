Ondo State AIDS, Viral Hepatitis and STI Control Program

Promoting health, providing care, and preventing the spread of HIV and STIs in Ondo State.

About the Program

The Ondo State AIDS, Viral Hepatitis, and STI Control Program (SASCP) is dedicated to reducing the impact of HIV/AIDS, viral hepatitis, and sexually transmitted infections (STIs) in Ondo State, Nigeria. Our mission is to improve public health through education, prevention, testing, treatment, and support services, with a special focus on the Prevention of Mother-to-Child Transmission (PMTCT) of HIV.

Our Approach

  • Awareness Campaigns: Reducing stigma and promoting early testing.
  • Community Outreach: Delivering tailored programs to vulnerable groups.
  • PMTCT Services: Ensuring healthy outcomes for mothers and babies.

SASCP is committed to empowering communities with the resources and care needed to prevent infections, manage health, and build a healthier future for all.
